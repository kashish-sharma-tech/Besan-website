What is Besan?

बेसन – The Golden Flour
of Indian Kitchens

Besan, also known as Gram Flour or Chickpea Flour, is a finely milled flour made from dried Chana Dal (split chickpeas). It is one of the most versatile and nutritious flours used in Indian cooking for centuries.

🥗

Culinary Uses

Used to make pakoras, kadhi, besan cheela, sev, ladoo, halwa, batter for bhajias, and countless more Indian delicacies — besan is the backbone of Indian snacks and sweets.

💪

Nutritional Powerhouse

Per 100g: ~360 kcal | Protein: 15.8g | Carbs: 50.6g | Dietary Fibre: 1.9g | Fat: 4.7g. High protein makes it ideal for vegetarians and fitness-conscious individuals.

🌿

Health Benefits

Naturally gluten-free, rich in plant-based protein, helps regulate blood sugar, supports digestion, and is loaded with iron, folate and magnesium for overall health.

🔬

NRS Purity Promise

NRS Besan is made exclusively from 100% Chana Dal — no mixing, no adulteration. Our special milling process preserves nutrients and ensures fine texture with low oil absorption.
